
# Endocrine System

## Glands

*   **Exocrine Glands:** Glands that secrete their products into ducts that lead to the target organ.
*   **Endocrine Glands:** Ductless glands that secrete hormones directly into the bloodstream.

## Hormone

**Definition:** A chemical substance produced in the body that controls and regulates the activity of certain cells or organs.

## Hormonal Glands

| Gland | Location | Secretion | Function |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Thyroid** | Neck | Thyroxine | Regulates metabolism |
| **Adrenal** | Above kidneys | Adrenaline | Prepares the body for 'fight or flight' response |
| **Pancreas** | Abdomen | Insulin, Glucagon | Regulates blood sugar levels |
| **Pituitary** | Brain | Growth hormone, TSH, etc. | Controls other endocrine glands |

## Adolescence and Accompanying Changes

*   **Physical Changes:** Growth spurt, development of secondary sexual characteristics.
*   **Emotional Changes:** Mood swings, increased self-consciousness.

## Personal Hygiene

*   **Importance:** To prevent the spread of disease and maintain good health.

    *   Meditation
    *   Time management
    *   Sports
